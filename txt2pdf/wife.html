Are you learning English and do you need help with grammar? Do you want to learn new vocabulary or slang (俚语)? Well, you can find help online with these top 5 free English as a Second Language websites.
1. Dave’s ESL Cafe
This is a complete website that can help you learn and practice English. You can read many stories and do a lot of exercises to test yourself. You can also post questions and answers onto the help center.
2. About.com
You can get free English courses from About.com and you’ll get regular e-mails that will help you learn English.
3. The Classroom by Voice of America
You can watch videos and listen to news stories to improve your listening and vocabulary skills. You can also hear English words and their meanings.
4. Heinle’s Newbury House Dictionary of American English
Do you need to find the definition of a word? Use this online dictionary to find simple and clear definitions, as well as example sentences and idioms (习语). You can also find different cultural facts.
5. Personal English Portal (门户网站) on YouTube
You can watch this series of videos on YouTube to develop your listening skills. You can also do a search for “learn English” on YouTube to find other videos. This is a good way to practice English and have fun.

【推荐1】   Spring is coming. People in different countries like different outdoor activities.

Paul, a reporter, interviewed some students. Here is what they have said.

Monica

(13, America)

I want to go out of the city to a beautiful field. It's happy to smell beautiful flowers, touch green grass and take some photos. And I like to have a picnic in the field. It’s interesting!

Greg

(14, Canada)

The weather turns warmer and the days become longer in spring. It's a good time to go camping. It would be fun to sleep in a tent. I enjoy the comfortable weather too.

John

 (13, England)

In spring, I like going cycling alone. It can not only make me enjoy the beautiful season but also help me become stronger. I can write something about what I see on the way.

Zhang Lan

(12, China)

I'm good at making all kinds of kites. And in spring, I like flying kites with my family or my friends in the park. One of us holds the kite and another one catches the line. It's exciting to see the kite flying high in the sky.

 

 
【小题1】Monica maybe lives in the _______.
A．townB．cityC．fieldD．countryside
【小题2】Greg thinks it fun to _______.
A．sleep in a tentB．take some photos
C．fly kitesD．have a picnic
【小题3】John likes going cycling in spring because _______.
A．he can enjoy the nice weatherB．it can make his body strong
C．his bike is very coolD．both A and B
【小题4】Zhang Lan always flies kites _______.
A．by herselfB．on rainy days
C．with her family or friendsD．in the field
【小题5】Which one is WRONG according to the passage?
A．Greg is the oldest one of the four.B．Monica is from England.
C．Making kites is Zhang Lan’s hobby.D．John is interested in writing.

【推荐2】   It is Sunday today. I don’t go to school. So I go to the shops with Mum. We have lunch at a small food shop. I put down my bag and sit at table. We have some rice, meat and vegetables. I drink a glass of apple juice, too. It’s one thirty in the afternoon. We want to come back home. But I can’t find my bag. It isn’t behind the chair now. At this time the shop owner (店主) comes in.

“Do you have a good lunch?” he asks.

“Yes, thanks a lot. But I can’t find my bag.” I say.

The owner goes out, and then he comes back with my bag. He gives it to me and says, “I’m sorry. My dog takes it to my room. He likes doing this.”

【小题1】My mother and I go to the shops on ______.
A．Sunday morningB．Saturday morningC．Sunday afternoon
【小题2】At a small food shop I don’t have ______.
A．juice or riceB．meat or vegetablesC．orange or milk
【小题3】I put my bag ______.
A．under the tableB．on the floorC．behind the chair
【小题4】When we want to leave the food shop, my bag is ______.
A．in the owner’s roomB．behind the chairC．in a dog’s mouth
【小题5】From the passage (文章) we know the shop owner ______.
A．is a greedy (贪婪的) manB．likes my bag very muchC．knows his dog very much

【推荐3】   Today is Friday. Peter has six classes today. He goes to school at 7:30 a. m. At 8:00 a. m., he begins his first class. The first class is English. At 9:00 a.m., he has Maths. Peter likes Maths because it's interesting. At 10:00 a. m., he has a biology class. Peter thinks it's very boring. Then at 11:00 a. m., he has a history class. That's his favourite subject. He can learn a lot about the past from History.

Peter has lunch at school at about 12:00. Then he has a short break. At 1:30 p.m., he has a music class. ` At 2:30 p.m., he has a PE class. Peter likes this afternoon, because Music and PE are easy and interesting. School is over at 4:00 p.m. Peter is happy because he can go home and have a good time on Saturday and Sunday.

【小题1】The second class is          on Friday morning.
A．ChineseB．EnglishC．MathsD．PE
【小题2】Peter likes          best in all his subjects.
A．MathsB．HistoryC．MusicD．Biology
【小题3】The underlined word "break" means         .
A．restB．timeC．gameD．class
【小题4】Peter likes this afternoon because         .
A．he likes Art and PE classesB．he has a history class
C．he has no classD．the classes are easy and interesting
【小题5】Which is TRUE?
A．Peter gets up at 7:30 in the morning.B．Peter thinks Maths is difficult and boring.
C．Peter doesn't have lunch at home on Friday.D．Peter is not happy on Friday afternoon.
